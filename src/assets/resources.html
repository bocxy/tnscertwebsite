Website Resource of TNSCERT

The TNSCERT youtube channel is linked there are nearly about 1500 video lessons which are categorized according to subject, standard, and topic. Whoever wants to view a specific video they can search typing the subject to standard and topic and retrieve it.
Under the resources, there are specific resources for teachers – like teacher’s manuals for CCE. Under student resources, online text books for Std I to XII and source books for (D.El. Education). There are also CCE worksheets for students, of I to IX standard. There are modules for std X and XII regarding career guidance, it has a link to the website www.indiacollegefinder.org
